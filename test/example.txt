Parser:
 - block: variable+
variable: GLOBAL? ID EQ expr SEMICOLON
expr: add | neg
neg: ADD mul
add: (add ADD mul) | mul
mul: (mul MUL pow) | pow
pow: (pow POW atom) | atom
paren: LPAREN expr RPAREN
atom: NUM | ID | paren


Lexer:
GLOBAL: `global`
ID: `[a-zA-Z_][a-zA-Z0-9_]*`
SEMICOLON: `;`
EQ: `=`
NUM: `[0-9]+`
ADD: `\+|\-`
MUL: `\*|\/`
POW: `\^`
LPAREN: `\(`
RPAREN: `\)`
